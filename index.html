About

I am a technology enthusiast, especially interested in web development, robotics, and IoT applications.
I find a special pleasure in learning and researching emerging technologies and how to help develop applications that make people's lives easier.
I'm a life-long learner who aspires to master his skills.
I've developed and implemented prototypes focused on industrial automation and IoT using open-source technologies such as Raspberry Pi, Arduino, etc.
Then I transitioned into a new discipline, web development.
I made websites, take courses, and then, I joined into Microverse where I've been working side by side with web developers around the world, developing real-life projects.
My current stack includes Ruby on Rails, React, Redux, ES6, Node.js, PostgreSQL, MySQL, Git, GithubFlow, DS, and Algorithms.
I’m looking for opportunities to bring that passion to a full-time role as a developer.
